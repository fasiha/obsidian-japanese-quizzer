# Wago Numbers

The ten native Japanese numbers (和語数詞, wago sūshi). These appear in everyday conversation and literary writing. Unlike the Sino-Japanese numbers (いち、に、さん…), wago numbers only go up to ten as a productive series, and they are commonly used with the general-purpose counter 〜つ.
